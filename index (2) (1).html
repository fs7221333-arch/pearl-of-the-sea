Our Story
✦

Where the Red Sea
Meets the Plate

Founded in 2009 along the pristine shores of Duba, Pearl of the Sea was born from a single obsession: to honour the extraordinary bounty of the Red Sea with the reverence it deserves. Our fishermen sail at dawn, our chefs light the grills at noon, and our guests arrive to a spectacle.

Every dish is a dialogue between tradition and technique — whole grilled Najdi snapper, clay-pot seafood bisques slow-cooked for six hours, and lobsters dressed simply in clarified butter and sea salt from the Farasan Islands.
